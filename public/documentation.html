How to use RecMate
First you will enter the website on the main page. If you scroll down on this page you will find a map with a list of all the available recreational centers located to the right of the map. You should be able to scroll through this list and click on your desired location.
Once you click on a recreational center, the map will drop a pin on this location and below the map this display of the recreational center's name and address will appear. To continue the process from here click the button saying book now.
After you click the “book now” button under the map, this pop-up will be displayed on the screen. This will then ask you for your information. Once finished, click the sumbit button prompting the website to reserve their time and specific recreation center.
Once you have filled out the booking information and submitted it, this screen will pop up. You will get an email to confirm that the booking was successful. This is done so that you know that the recreational center of their choosing has received the booking information.
