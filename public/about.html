ABOUT US:
Each and every day people of all ages try their best to get outside and be active. People have the option to run outside, pay for a gym membership, or even workout in their own homes. For the people who get their workout in by playing a sport, these options arent always the most efficient. Most sports can be played on the front lawn or in the backyard, however there are many times where a court/field is needed or people simply do not have enough space to conduct a game. Our application aims to help people in your community find open fields for all sports. Not only is the application helping people find open fields, but it is bringing the community together. If people knew precise information about the frequently unused recreational centers in their area they would be more inclined to use them. As a team we discussed why this application is so important. We all have a passion for sports but have faced a difficult time finding open courts and enough players to field a game. We believe that this application will tackle that issue and promote physical excercise while bringing people together.
